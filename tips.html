Tips & Chess Principles

Never stop learning in chess—every game brings a new lesson. Study your losses, celebrate your creative moves, and enjoy the journey to mastery!
  • Control the center (e4, d4, e5, d5) early.
  • Develop your knights and bishops efficiently.
  • Castle early to protect your king.
  • Don’t bring your queen out too soon in the opening.
  • Always pay attention to checks, captures, and threats on every move.
  • Connect your rooks and aim to place them on open files.
  • Every move must have a purpose—play with a plan!
